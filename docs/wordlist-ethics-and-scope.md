# Wordlist Ethics and Scope

Wordlists are training and testing material. They should be curated for authorized labs, CTFs, classroom ranges, and contracted assessments.

## Allowed

- Synthetic lists.
- Public challenge lists.
- Technology-specific test strings.
- Local app route dictionaries.
- Defensive validation lists for owned systems.

## Blocked

- Private credential dumps.
- Personal data collections.
- Harassment lists.
- Real account stuffing workflows.
- Spray-and-pray public-target activity.

## FLLC positioning

Free content should explain how to choose a list responsibly. Basic content can include reporting worksheets and toy target labs. Premium content can include guided authorized assessment workflows with rate limits, logs, and remediation evidence.
